Lasagna

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, combining layers of rich flavors and textures into a hearty, satisfying dish. This classic recipe begins with a savory meat sauce simmered to perfection, blending ground beef, Italian sausage, tomatoes, and aromatic herbs. A creamy ricotta mixture, infused with Parmesan and fresh parsley, adds a velvety richness, while perfectly cooked lasagna noodles provide structure and substance.Each layer is topped with shredded mozzarella, creating a gooey, golden crust as the lasagna bakes to bubbly perfection. This dish is ideal for family dinners, gatherings, or meal prep, as it tastes even better the next day. Serve it with a crisp green salad and garlic bread for a complete Italian-inspired feast that will leave everyone at the table asking for seconds.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cove with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
